# LedgerHouse Phase 1 Scope Exclusions

As specified in the LedgerHouse Business and Technical Plan, Phase 1 is a tightly controlled pilot. The following features are explicitly excluded from the Phase 1 scope:

- **Open Banking**: No live bank feeds in Phase 1 (scheduled for months 7–9, using an FCA-authorised aggregator before broad direct sales).
- **PDF Bank-Statement Extraction**: Bank imports are strictly CSV-based with structured templates.
- **MTD VAT Filing**: No direct HMRC API VAT filings. Filings remain with the client's accountant until Phase 3.
- **Payroll Processing**: No payroll calculations or processing within LedgerHouse.
- **Statutory Accounts**: No preparation of statutory annual accounts.
- **Corporation Tax**: No corporation tax computation or returns.
- **Payment Execution**: LedgerHouse does not execute payments or connect to payment gateways for outgoing funds.
- **Credit Control**: No credit control chasing or automated collections.
- **Cash-Flow Forecasting**: No financial forecasting or projection modules.
- **Natural-Language Accounting Questions**: No ledger chat or conversational AI interfaces.
- **White-Label Console**: Deferred until at least two firm partners commit and define requirements.
- **Multi-Currency**: GBP functional and transaction currency only in Phase 1.
- **Client-Money Handling**: No holding or management of client funds.
- **Broad General-Market Sale**: Distribution is restricted to design partners and direct pilots in the beachhead care/supported housing sectors.
